Horn Antenna Design (10 GHz)

Given:
f = 10 GHz
a = 2.286 cm, b = 1.016 cm
G0 = 20 dB

1. Gain (linear):
   G = 10^(G0/10) = 100

2. Wavelength:
   λ = c/f = 3e8 / 1e10 = 0.03 m = 3 cm

3. Cutoff freq (TE10):
   fc = c/(2a) ≈ 6.56 GHz

4. X1:
   X1 = G / (2π√(2π)) = 6.345

5. ρe:
   ρe = X1·λ = 6.345 × 3 = 19.035 cm

6. ρh:
   ρh = (G² / (8π³)) (λ / ρe) ≈ 19.1 cm

7. Aperture:
   a1 = √(3λρh) = 13.1 cm
   b1 = √(2λρe) = 10.69 cm

8. Gain check:
   G = (4πa1b1)/λ² ≈ 97.7 ≈ 20 dB
